<b>Cecep Syamsul Hari</b> (born on May 1, 1967 in Bandung, West Java) is an Indonesian poet. He is the editor of <i>Horison Monthly</i> literary magazine. Besides poems, he has published selected essays, critics, short-stories, a fictocriticism novel, and some translation-works. His works had been translated into English, German, Korea, Bengali, Portugal, and Czech.
